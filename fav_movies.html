My Favorite Movie List

Anbe Sivam

ANBE SIVAM is a Tamil drama about two strangers-Nallasivam(Kamal Haasan) and Anbarasu(Madhavan)-who travel together after a flight gets cancelled.Through the journey,Anbaras learns about Nallasivam's painful past,his beliefs,and his kindness.The film shows that love and humanity are the true form of god,reflecting the idea that "Anbe Sivam"means Love is god.

Abhiyum Nanum

ABHIYUM NANUM is a Tamil family drama that focuses on the loving relationship between a father,Raghuram(Prakash raj),and his daughter Abhi(Trisha).As Abhi grows up,Raghuram struggles to accept her independence and her choices-especially when she decides to marry someone he never expected.The film beautifully shows a father's emotions,love,fears,and the journey of learning to let go.It hightlights how parents must understand and trust their children as they step into their own lives.

Kannathil Muthamittal

Kannathil Muthamittal is a Tamil drama about a young girl adopted by loving parents who sets out to find her biological mother in war-torn Sri Lanka,exploring identity,love,and family bonds.
